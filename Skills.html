Capstone Project

https://web-projects.net/global-institute/ My experience with the project was mostly negative during the process. Working with a theme can be very frustrating. There are a lot of things to work around and it was a struggle at times. Lots of targeting needed to be done if we wanted to change anything. One thing I have had to deal with so potently before is a busy information distributor. Our client was a busy man so if you needed some information, then you needed to contact him far in advance. One thing I learned is that my tendency to shut down really affects the person I'm interacting with. My project lead was sometimes at a loss as to what to do with me at some points. Similar to a previous experience I've had. In the future I'm going to try and be mindful of when it's happening. Another thing that I learned about was communication. The team did not communicate what they had worked on, so at times one of our team members became stressed. After that I made an effort to update them on what I worked on following that meeting. Once the project was over and after all the stress, after all the work, after talking with the client and hearing how important this was for them, it made me feel for a moment like maybe it was worth it, which is the last thing I learned. The time I worked the hardest and appreciated the project the most is when others expressed how important it was to them.
